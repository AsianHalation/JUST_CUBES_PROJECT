# JUST_CUBES_PROJECT
Spanish - Dutch collaboration

## Project Approach
The project is organised as a collaborative learning experience. Students work in international mixed teams and learn through peer learning, experimentation and problem solving.

Teachers do not give traditional lessons but act as facilitators and coaches who support the teams during the project process.

Students are encouraged to learn from each other, expirement with digital tools and artificial intelligence, divide roles within their teams and present and discuss their ideas with other groups.

## Central Project Assignment
### Build an AI powered Dropshipping Store
Students work in international teams consisting of thw Dutch students and two Spanish or Basque students.

Each team develops a small digital business concept. The teams choose a niche market and select a product that could be sold usting the dropshipping model. Students design a simple online store and use AI tools to support product research, product descriptions and branding. In addition, teams develop an idea for automation that could improve the webshop or customer experience. at the end of the project each team presents its concept in a short pitch.

The project sessions take place daily from 9:30 to 13:00

**The final output consists of a demo webshop, a short business concept and a five minute presentation.**

## General Approach
### Day 1 Netherlands
Introduction and briefing of product research and target audience

### Day 2 Netherlands - Store Concept and Content Creation
**Theme Designing the webshop**
Focus on the home and product pages and how costumers will navigate through the webshop.
- Product descriptions
- marketing text
- Frequently asked Question
- Automation that could improve the webshop or customer experiencce

At the end of the day, teams have a clear store concept, marketing content and a visual design

### Day 3 Netherlands - Prototype Development
**Theme building the store**
Goal is to create a working concept or prototype website rather than a fully functional online store. You can use tools such as Shopify, Wordpress, Simple HTML pages or visual mock up tools.

### Day 4 Netherlands - Startup Pitcj and Reflection
**Theme presenting ideas**
Teams prepare their startup pitch. Each teams presents its product idea, webshop concept, the way AI was used and the automation idea they developed. They also explain the potential market and the balue of their product.

Each team presents for about five minutes. Other students ask questions and give feedback.

During the final part of the session students participate in a reflection activity. They discuss what they learned during the project, what surprised them and what they would improve in the future.

The day ends with a cultural activity in the city.
