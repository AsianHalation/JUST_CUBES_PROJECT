THE MATERIALS

Walnut Wood

Sustainably sourced American black walnut, hand-sanded to 2000-grit and finished with natural oil.

Brushed Steel

316L stainless steel, CNC machined to ±0.01mm tolerance, brushed to a matte satin finish.

Carrara Marble

Hand-cut from Italian Carrara marble blocks, each piece unique with natural veining patterns.

Optical Glass

Borosilicate optical glass, kiln-cast and cold-worked to optical clarity with zero bubbles.

Vulcanized Rubber

Premium natural rubber, hot-vulcanized for exceptional durability and a tactile matte surface.

Cast Resin

UV-stable epoxy resin with embedded botanicals, minerals, or pigments — each cast is one of a kind.

WHY A CUBE?

The cube is the purest expression of three-dimensional form. Six equal faces, twelve equal edges, eight equal vertices. No object contains more perfect symmetry in less visual complexity.

We make cubes because they demand honesty from materials. Wood grain wraps around sharp corners. Light refracts through glass edges. Metal radiates from brushed planes. The cube reveals everything.
